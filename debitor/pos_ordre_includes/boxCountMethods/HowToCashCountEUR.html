How to do a cash register count

Step 1

Count all the cash in the register, either by entering the number of coins/bills
into the fields, or by entering the total amount in "Other EUR."

Step 2

Press the Calculate button

Step 3

Please note that "Taken from checkout field" can be changed.

For example, if you want there to be EUR 200 left in the till,

and the calculated balance is EUR 300, EUR 100 must be entered in
Withdraw from cash register".

For more guidance:

https://saldiregnskab.dk/saldi-support/kasseoptaelling
